Pitch Deck Agency FAQs

Pitch Deck Design FAQs

Here are some FAQs and answers to ease your mind.

How many slides should a pitch deck have?

A pitch deck can have anywhere from 10 to 25+ slides, depending on the complexity of the business and the goals of the pitch. It’s important to keep the pitch deck concise and focused on the most important information.

Can you provide examples of pitch decks you've developed for other clients?
Yes, we can provide examples of pitch decks we’ve developed for other clients upon request. These can give you an idea of the style and format of our pitch decks and how we’ve helped other businesses effectively communicate their value proposition.
How long does it take to develop a pitch deck?

The time it takes to develop a pitch deck can vary depending on the complexity of the business and the goals of the pitch. Typically, it takes about 1-2 weeks for a basic deck and 1-2 months for a more complex one.
