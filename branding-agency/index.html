Our branding process

Branding Agency FAQs

Branding FAQs

Here are some FAQs and answers to ease your mind.

How Much Does Branding Cost?

There’s no fixed cost for brand identity design, it depends completely on the client’s needs and the branding agency. Some companies will spend as little as $/£3,000, while others will spend as much as $/£50,000.

I Have An Urgent Timeline. Can You Achieve It?
Yes, we can! Our typical lead time is 8-12 weeks, but this can be expedited to as little as 2-4 weeks thanks to our US, EMEA & APAC delivery teams.
What If I Just Want A Logo?
No problem, all of our packages are entirely bespoke and built around your needs.
What Sectors Do You Create Brands For?
Our agency has the most experience in tech, business & financial services, but we work with companies in all sectors to build show-stopping brands.
