5 Smart Website Value Tools to Evaluate Your Website in 2022

There’s no doubt about the importance of having a website for your business. In today’s world, if you don’t have any digital presence and custom website development then you’re not going to make it to the top. Website is an asset that directly communicates your message to the audience and ensures that you’re seen by your customers. Gone are days when you had to put billboards and run TV and newspaper ads to reach people because social media is the king today.

However, having a website is not enough. Using a website value tool is necessary to see if your website is performing well on the internet. Unless you’re aware of your website’s shortcomings, you won’t be able to earn from it.

But, what if you decide to sell your website? How much money will you get for it? How will you check the selling price of the website?

All of these questions can be tempting, but thanks to the website value tools that will help you to answer all of these questions.

The selling price of your website is dependent on multiple factors. For instance, the age of the website, its type, and so much more. Before you put a price on your website, it’s important to evaluate its worth, so buyers don’t find your selling price unreasonable. To avoid the long haul, here are some intelligent website worth evaluators that can help you to calculate your website’s selling price.

5 Smart Website Valuation Tools

Don’t look further if you’re trying to find website valuation tools. Here’s a list of them.

GoDaddy

One of the best website valuation tools is GoDaddy that is easy to use and gives accurate results.

It checks if the website name contains any specific keywords and how many are other similar websites are sold to give the correct estimation.

One of the best things about using this tool for website value estimates is because it tells the grounds on which your website is priced. For instance, it tells you about the reasons why your website is priced high or low. It further tells you if there are any other websites available for purchase, so you can instantly purchase another cheaper option without wasting your time.

It’s not necessary to use this tool only when you’re selling your website. You can check your website’s estimated prices at GoDaddy without having to sell it. It gives you a fair idea of how much money you can bring in by selling this website to the right party. So, it’s a good option for people who plan on selling their website in the future. Know that this estimate won’t take your business and website’s performance into account.

Flippa

Another great website value tool that you can use for website value estimates is Flippa. It’s one of the most popular tools for purchasing and selling websites. It’s an excellent and most-used tool for website valuation that will give you a clear picture of how much you will get paid for your website. If you’re looking to sell your website, then this is the tool that you should use.

If, after checking the selling price of your website on Flippa, you decide to sell it, then you can list it down on Flippa for free. Flippa will further tell you why your website is priced at this amount, so you have a clear picture of everything. It’s a free consultation that will give you a detailed insight into your website. All this information is further helpful in improving the value of your website through evaluation to work on it and get better pricing. Once you have all the details, you can work on improving your website’s performance in no time.

Estibot

Just like the name predicts, this website is all about estimation. The tools allow you to value your website’s value while considering a few factors including cost-per-click, the keywords used for optimization, and so much more. All of these factors play a great role in estimating the website’s value, so you can expect an appropriate answer from this tool.

One reason why this website valuation tool is trusted by people is because of the unique way through which it finds the right selling price for your website. For instance, it takes the website traffic, ranking, and everything else into consideration for the job. By using this tool, you can come across all the details necessary to analyze your website. Staying aware of all the metrics will enable you to check the value of your website. All you have to do is register on this platform and use it free of cost.

AdSense

If you want to know about your website’s potential, then Google AdSense is one of the best tools for it. You cannot sell your website at the right price if it’s not performing well and you can evaluate its performance through Google AdSense.

AdSense is a tool by SEMRush, which plays a key role in identifying your website’s potential. When you’re buying or selling a website, then the information collected from this tool can help you make a wise decision.

If you want your website to perform well and wish to sell it at a higher price, then optimizing it for relevant and specific keywords is a great way to do it. Sometimes, after going through the collected information, the business owner decides to optimize the website to sell it later at a higher price. The market value of your website can increase drastically if you’re using the information to improve your website’s performance.

Ahrefs

Ahrefs helps in analyzing the website’s traffic to give you a value estimation for your website. It’s an SEO tool, which is simple and easy to use. It gives out correct information, so you can make an informed decision about selling your website.

Simply, create an account on Ahrefs, log in, and check your website’s worth by only entering the domain name. Ahrefs gives you information based on the traffic on your website along with checking the website’s SEO.

You can check the website’s potential along with analyzing it for other websites too. Before using this tool, make sure that you have secured all relevant information regarding the website. For instance, you have records of your investments made on it, the revenue that the website generated, and so on. You must also be able to change the ownership of the website, so there are no issues in selling it to the right party.

Value My Web

Another famous tool that can help you in estimating your website’s value is Value My Web that uses more information as compared to other tools. Their approach is unique, which is why it’s on the list.

They go through a lot of data before finally giving their verdict on the website’s value. For instance, they go through the website’s social media, keywords, SEO, and other information to decide its worth. If you’re looking for a trusted website for website valuation estimation, then this is the one for you!

Cubestat

Along with others, Cubestat is another website value estimation tool that tells you about your website’s value in the market. Cubestat checks all information related to your website along with checking daily page views, and ad revenue generated on the website.

This information lets you see how your website is performing in the market, so you can either opt for a different strategy or decide to sell your website at the same price.

Glurk.com

Last on the list of website value tool is Glurk that lets you estimate the value of your website. You can base your judgment on this tool because it asks for a lot of intangibles that affect your website’s performance. Know that, you will be entering the information in this tool, so make sure that you’re entering the right information. The tool further checks the income source, pronunciation, domain name length, and so on to give an estimated value of your website.

Few Tips to Successfully Sell Your Website

Here are some tips that will help you sell your website without any hindrance.

  • When selling your website, think of its long-term value in the market. Don’t rely on monthly or weekly profits only as it will only bring you losses. Your website has potential, then it’s definitely worth the price you put on it. For instance, if your website is a hit on social media and is continuously witnessing growth in its audience, then it will definitely sell at a high price. Fix a price after considering all aspects of your website.

 

  • Trying to know your audience will save you from the trouble when you’re selling your website. The same idea won’t apply to all the websites; therefore, you have to come up with a different strategy every time you’re trying to sell a website. If you have listed your website on Flippa, know that people are more interested in knowing your website’s profits and generated revenue there. Knowing your audience will tell you how your audience will react to the auction.

 

  • Another important tip is to look at other sales before going with your gut feeling. By checking those websites, you will get an idea as to where your website is lacking and how you can sell it at a higher price. It will further help you to find out about the tactics and pitches to auction your website at the right price. It can significantly improve the value of your website and will help you to place your website at a good price for sale.

 

  • Placing your website for sale without optimizing it for profits is the biggest mistake that you will make. Even your audience is looking to purchase a website that is optimized enough to bring them revenue, so why not invest your time in it? Test different monetization methods utilize lead capture strategies, and so on to gain a huge price while selling your website. If you’re not making the effort, then there is a clear chance that you will face a big loss.

 

  • Although there are hundreds of options to sell your website, you have to choose the right one to attract buyers. For instance, Flippa is famous for selling websites and listing them down for buyers and sellers. Using a popular outlet for website auctions will save you from the trouble of standing on a waiting list.

Utilize these tips to sell your website fast to the right people.

Closing Thoughts

If you’re waiting for a long time to sell your website, then the above-mentioned tips will accelerate the process for you. Before selling your website, don’t forget to check its value by using a few website valuation tools to put the right price on your website. Don’t be in a hurry to sell your website and check how you can improve your website’s value to gain more profit over it.
