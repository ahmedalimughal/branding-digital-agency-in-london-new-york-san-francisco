What steps do we follow for

video production?

Concept development

Who is your audience? What do you plan to accomplish with the video? What message is meant to go across? The initial phase involving rigorous concept development.

Script writing

Writing a script that aligns with the targeted video time, and any changes that might be required to add in camera angle, background, and wardrobe.

Pre-production

During the pre-production planning we decide the appropriate locations, all the equipment and props required, script reviewing, deciding the budget, and finalizing the date and time.

Video shoot

Executing everything planned in the pre-production phase, we ensure everything from the actors’ availability to technical personnel and final delivery are smooth and flawless.

Editing

We have professional video editors on board to polish the explainers, whiteboards, and animations and special effects in a manner client has asked.

Coding

Coding the video in the best way possible for it to enjoy maximum distribution and prominence across all the major smartphones, tablets, and desktop.

Distribution

Conducting video SEO in terms of optimized meta titles and descriptions, selecting the right video category, and using captions for effective distribution.

Marketing

Promoting your video on relevant social platforms, publishing in press releases, email newsletter, and guest blogs for gathering optimum reach and engagement.

FAQs

Have questions before hiring Tech4States?

Here is what you need to know!

What is your video production process?

Creative and engaging videos are an asset for your content and we make sure to deliver the best. We listen to each and every detail you have related to the production and create the best animation with our abilities. We start the process with understanding your needs, storyboard, voiceover, illustration, animation, and last delivery. We keep you posted at every step,

How long does an animated video cost?

A normal video is between 50 to 60 seconds long and it takes around 4 to 6 weeks to complete it. If your project requires special attention then we will be needing a larger team and that may take up to 10 weeks.

How many revisions do I get?

We discuss your needs before starting anything hence you might not need any revisions. But if you do, you can have unlimited revisions for script development. After this stage revisions will be limited.

How many people will work on my video?

A video team consists of a story lead, storyboard artist, professional voiceover, illustrator, animator, and sound designer. All these people will be highly professionals and experts in their fields.

How long should my video be?

A typical 60 seconds video is enough to deliver your message. But a video can be as long as you want according to your topic.
