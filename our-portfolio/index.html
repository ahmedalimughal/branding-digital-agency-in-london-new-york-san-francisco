Have questions before hiring Tech4States?

Here is what you need to know!

What is your new client onboarding process?

After the client is taken on board, we assign a dedicated project manager and product strategist for your project. Then we’ll start the kickoff meetings discussing your project scope, expectations, and process minutiae.

What types of clients do you work with?

Tech4States’s major pool of clientele is from B2B sector—companies with 25-250 employees, making $5-50 million in revenue wanting to improve their online reputation management and acquire a comprehensive set of branding and development services.

Our clients are startups, SMBs, aspiring entrepreneurs, and enterprises from different industries including real estate, healthcare, gaming, media, transportation, entertainment, etc.

What services do you provide?

Tech4States is a creative marketing agency offering services in ecommerce web development, mobile app development, digital marketing, SEO services and design consultancy.

What is your pricing model?

Every client we take on board is specific, both in nature and budget of the project. Hence, we don’t use a one-size-fits-all philosophy in our pricing model.
