Our web design process

We’ve refined our process as a New York-based web design agency through designing & developing websites for years.

Let’s Meet

We want to get to the bottom of what makes you tick. We base all we do on your beliefs, expertise, and company history.

Market Research

We immerse ourselves in your industry, conducting in-depth competitor website evaluations to understand your position and strategize how we can elevate your presence above the rest.​

Moodboard Dreamin’

A mission without a vision is incomplete, and our designers excel in this realm. We'll craft website moodboards for your scrutiny, ensuring your concept is manifested splendidly.​

Site Mapping

How can we direct your New York and worldwide visitors? We enlist the help of our digital architects to meticulously plan out all site pages and sections so that we can map out your path to success.

Magnetic Copywriting

This is the thrilling part. We start by putting pen to paper (or fingers to keyboard) and honing your copy before we start creating great content for you. ​

Figma UI Design

Now things start to come together. We start bringing your plan and vision to life and create the website UI in Figma, building the foundations of your brand new digital home.

Website Development

Our web development wizards get to work recreating the site's user interface in WordPress, Shopify, HubSpot, or any other CRM, adding animation, and developing custom features.

SEO Sorcery

Prepare to Google. We're not your typical web design firm; SEO is in our blood. Our technical staff works its magic to ensure that your site ranks well in all search engines and is ready to be noticed both in and outside of New York.

Quality Assurance

Testing boards are set up. We diligently examine every facet of the website to guarantee its flawless functionality and readiness for the audience.​

Analytics And Data Setup

Do you have a taste for delectable data? We'll install your choice analytics program so you can monitor the site's performance at every stage.

Web Agency FAQs

Web Design FAQs

Here are some FAQs and answers to ease your mind.

How much does web design cost?

There’s no fixed cost for web design, it depends completely on the client’s needs. Some companies will spend as little as $/£4,000 for a simple site, while others will spend as much as $/£100,000.

I have an urgent timeline. Can you achieve it?
Yes, we can! Our typical lead time is 8-12 weeks, but this can be expedited to as little as 2-4 weeks thanks to our US, EMEA & APAC delivery teams.
Do you offer ongoing website maintenance and support?
Yes! We offer lightning-fast hosting and ongoing website management, security and SEO packages.
Do you create custom-designed websites?
Every website we create is custom designed in Figma before being developed in the client’s CMS of choice.
