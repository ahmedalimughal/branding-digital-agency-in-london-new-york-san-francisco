London Branding Agency FAQs

Branding FAQs

Here are some FAQs and answers to ease your mind.

How Much Does Branding Cost?

There is no set price for brand identity design. It all depends on what the client wants and what the branding agency offers. Some London businesses will spend as little as $/3,000, while others will spend as much as $/50,000.

I Have An Urgent Timeline. Can You Achieve It?

Yes, we can! Our typical lead time is 812 weeks, but our US, EMEA, and APAC delivery teams can shorten this to as little as 24 weeks.

What If I Just Want A Logo?
No problem, all of our packages are entirely bespoke and built around your needs.
What Sectors Do You Create Brands For?

Our agency has the most experience in technology, business, and financial services, but we work with companies from all industries to create eye-catching brands.
